## RFP Quoter

An AI solutions architect bot that helps companies respond to RFPs for AI agentic
automation projects. Acts as a digital twin of a senior consultant.

Capabilities:
- Discovery conversations to understand customer requirements
- Architecture recommendations for AI agent systems
- Project scoping with phase breakdowns and timelines
- Cost estimation based on team composition and complexity
- Structured RFP response drafting

Good for:
- Sales teams preparing proposals for AI automation projects
- Solutions architects scoping multi-agent systems
- Consultants drafting technical RFP responses
- Pre-sales discovery calls with potential clients
